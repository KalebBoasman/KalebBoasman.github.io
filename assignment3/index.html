World Summary

The world of Palea is undergoing a rapture. Half a century ago the Great Nations of the world sent their men off to die in the blistering deserts of the Divide, the blood of millions mixing with the currents of black gold running beneath the sands. Now the dust has settled and the people of Palea look inwards. It is an era of humanism, political theory, decolonisation and revolution. The actions of brave individuals, determined resistance movements and secret organisations will shape this era, not nations. To add to the cacophony of chaos engulfing Palea, a small population of gifted men and women are beginning to discover their hidden abilities - whether they use their newfound powers for good or evil remains to be seen.
